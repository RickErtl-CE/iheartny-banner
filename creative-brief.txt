Background:
The World Cup 2026 is a major event drawing fans from all over the nation and the
world. Beginning June 13, The New York/New Jersey stadium will host 8 matches -
including the biggest and most important one of all: the World Cup Final on July 19.
This presents a key opportunity to encourage visitors to explore New York State between
matches or extend their stay by highlighting the region’s diverse, world-class
destinations.

Objectives:
To promote World Cup visitors to explore New York City & New York State, and to
raise awareness about all the world-class attractions NY has to offer
To drive traffic to the ILNY FIFA World Cup landing page
Important distinction - it is not our goal to encourage World Cup ticket sales or travel
to the World Cup. Our goal is to encourage people to explore beyond the World Cup
events while they are here.

Target:
Group 1: Confirmed World Cup Ticket Holders (Domestic and International)
• U.S. based fans attending matches in New York, Philadelphia, and Boston
• International ticket holders from Brazil, France, England, and Germany
Group 2: World Cup Intenders (Domestic Only)
• U.S. based users actively searching for World Cup tickets for matches in New York
• Based on historical trends, World Cup ticket sales increase significantly as the event
approaches

Deliverables:
Two High Impact Carousels (see vendor specs on page 2)
• V1: Group 1 / Confirmed World Cup ticket holders — featuring more premium destinations
(e.g., the Hamptons)
• V2: Group 2 / World Cup intenders — highlighting more accessible destinations

Overall Messaging:
• This year, the world’s biggest sporting event will be right here in New York. So
come for the soccer, stay for the world-class sights, sounds and culture that
can only be found in New York State.
OR
• Come for the soccer, stay for New York. And beyond the pitch, discover
everything else you love that can only be found in New York State.
